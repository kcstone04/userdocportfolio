Assignment Information

This assignment was for me to conduct usability tests of my two user guides, the print user guide and the video user guide. I had two of my friends follow the guides to the best of their ability, and I observed how well it went. They gave me feedback at the end, which I noted down. The deliverables for this assignment were to make a script for each test, each with a selection of questions meant to guide the tests, and a consent form for each of my test users to sign.

What Did I Learn?

Conducting the usability tests were also pretty fun, mainly because my test participants were two of my close friends. Both of my friends were already proficient in using Steam, but they made an effort to follow the instructions from the guides without using their own knowledge. The friend who followed the print guide was especially committed to following the guide to the letter, and ended up only running into two minor issues.
