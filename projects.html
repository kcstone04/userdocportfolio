Print User Guide

This assignment was for me write a step-by-step guide to a simple task in my chosen software. The software I chose was Steam, the videogame marketplace and library application. This choice will extend into most of the other assignments. The task I chose was to simply find, purchase, and download a game – the basic task that most people use Steam for. The guide had to be well-written for people who have no idea how to use Steam, and it had to include images as visual aids.

Video User Guide

This assignment was for me to make a video guide walking users through a simple task in my software of choice, Steam. I decided to essentially make an adaptation of my print guide, walking users through the same task in a different guide format. This video required me to record a video of my screen and microphone, and ensure the guide was the proper length, and paced well to make it easy to follow.

Usability Test Documents

This assignment was for me to conduct usability tests of my two user guides, the print user guide and the video user guide. I had two of my friends follow the guides to the best of their ability, and I observed how well it went. They gave me feedback at the end, which I noted down. The deliverables for this assignment were to make a script for each test, each with a selection of questions meant to guide the tests, and a consent form for each of my test users to sign.
