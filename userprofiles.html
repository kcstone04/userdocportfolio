Assignment Information

This assignment was to lay out some basic, important information about my test participants. The profiles were anonymous, completely leaving out their names and other easily identifiable information. Profiles like this are useful to get an idea of the type of people you are writing your documentation for.

What Did I Learn?

This assignment was really easy. I didn’t properly gather all of the information for the profiles when I was conducting the tests, but since I knew them both fairly well I knew a lot of the info myself, and was able to ask about anything else I didn’t know.
