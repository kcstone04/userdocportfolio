Assignment Information

This assignment had me write a step-by-step guide to a simple task in my chosen software. The software I chose was Steam, the videogame marketplace and library application. This choice will extend into most of the other assignments. The task I chose was to simply find, purchase, and download a game – the basic task that most people use Steam for. The guide had to be well-written for people who have no idea how to use Steam, and it had to include images as visual aids.

What Did I Learn?

This project helped me practice writing simple, easy-to-follow instructions. It also involved taking screenshots and editing them to include additional visual instructions. This wasn’t part of the assignment instructions, but I enjoyed styling the guide to vaguely match Steam’s color scheme.
