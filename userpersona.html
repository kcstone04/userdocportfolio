Assignment Information

This assignment was to use pieces of info about my test participants, much of which was included in the user profiles, to create a detailed profile for an imaginary person. Personas like this can be practically useful as a way of combining information drawn from many, many individual profiles. For this assignment, it was just a combination of two people, so it wasn’t particularly representative of anything.

What Did I Learn?

I had a lot of fun with this assignment. I got a bit carried away, since it involved creating an amalgamation of two of my good friends. Thus I created Jalban Muman, a “perfect” combination of my two test participants. Unfortunately the text in the boxes got misaligned in pdf form, but oh well.
